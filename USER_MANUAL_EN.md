# Timestamp Camera Pro User Manual

Welcome to **Timestamp Camera Pro**, a premium camera application designed for professionals. It helps you capture photos and videos with complete and beautiful details of coordinates, time, and location.

---

## 1. Getting Started

When you open the app for the first time, the system will request necessary permissions. Please tap "Allow" for the app to work at its full potential:
*   **Camera**: To take photos and videos.
*   **Microphone**: To record audio in videos.
*   **Location**: To specify GPS coordinates and current address in the watermark (selecting "While using the app" is best).
*   **Notifications**: To display Google Drive upload status or video processing status.

> **Note**: If the compass is inaccurate, try moving the phone in a figure-8 motion in the air to calibrate the sensors.

## 3. Editor Mode

Not just for new shots, you can also add watermarks to old photos!
1.  Tap the **Image/Gallery** icon at the bottom left to view taken photos.
2.  Select the **"Import"** tab or icon.
3.  Choose a photo from the device Gallery.
4.  The app will display the photo with the current watermark (Date/Time will be from the original photo or current time, depending on settings).
5.  Press **Save** to save the new photo with the watermark to the device (does not overwrite the original file).

## 5. FAQ

**Q: Why doesn't the address show up?**
A: Check if GPS is enabled and Location permission is allowed for the app. In some areas without internet signal, the place name might not load, but GPS coordinates will always work.

**Q: Where are the photos stored?**
A: Photos are saved in the `Pictures/TimestampCamera` or `DCIM/TimestampCamera` folder on your device.

**Q: The watermark blocks the image, want to move it?**
A: Go to Settings > Watermark Design > Position and select a new position.
